VISION

The vision of the organisation is to have a Zambia with equal access to secure land tenure, decent, adequate, affordable housing and auxilliary services and economic opportunities for all.

MISSION

The organisation mission is to create a platform of civil society organisation addressing household poverty, lobbying and advocating for equal acess to secure land tenure, decent, adequate, affordable housing and auxilliary services and safe habitat for the rural and urban marginalised poor communities of Zambia.

Our Members

Build it International

Focuses on providing practical and sustainable response to the chronic need for basic infrastructure and jobs in sub - sahara Africa. In Zambia the organisation is building Schools, health facilities and housing with local materials and sustainable technologies with the aim of making communities access the facilities the need.

Habitat for Humanity

Habitat for Humanity Zambia driven by the love of Jesus Christ envisages a Zambia where everyone has a decent house to live in. The organisation operates three programs namely: Orphans and Vulnerable Groups housing services, education, shelter, water and sanitation, health, HIV/AIDS, OVC care activities and the Water, Sanitation and Hygiene Promotion program.

Katuba Women Association

Women led and focus on lobby and advocacy for equitable access to land by grassroots Women. The association provides paralegal services to women and girls who suffer all forms of abuse.

Bwalo Environmental Trust

Focuses on capacity building and fostering development among the poor and marginalised communities of Kafue.
It has a multifaceted approach in promotion of environmental health which includes improved housing for the inhabitants.
The organisation has been lobbying and advocating for adequate housing in the settlements of Simikobo, Chanyanya, Lukolongo and Zambia Compound which are informal settlements in Kafue District.
